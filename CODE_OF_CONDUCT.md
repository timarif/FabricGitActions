# Code of conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community.

Contributors and maintainers must:

- Communicate constructively and assume good intent.
- Respect differing experience levels, backgrounds, and viewpoints.
- Focus feedback on the work rather than the person.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, and disclosure of private
  information.

Unacceptable behavior may be removed, and participants may be temporarily or
permanently excluded from project spaces. Report conduct concerns privately to
the repository maintainers. Do not include confidential tenant or credential
information in a conduct report.
